Delete your data

Moofy doesn't have accounts — here's what that means, and how to permanently erase your data.

There's no "Moofy account" to delete. Moofy has no sign-up, no login, and no password — there's nothing stored on a server we control. Your data lives locally on your device and, if you've enabled it, in your own private iCloud account. Because of that, we don't have the ability to delete your data on your behalf, even if you ask us to — you're the only one with access to it, so deletion is something only you can do. The good news: that also makes it fast.

Option 1: Delete everything in the app

The fastest and most complete way — takes about 15 seconds.

  1. Open the Moofy app.
  2. Go to the Settings tab.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete All Data.
  4. Confirm by tapping Delete Everything in the warning that appears.
  5. Every transaction, budget, account, and goal is deleted immediately — on this device and, via sync, in your private iCloud database.

Option 2: Remove your iCloud data without the app

Lost your device, or already deleted the app? Your synced data lives in your own iCloud account, so you can remove it directly from your device's system settings — no Moofy app required.

  1. Open the Settings app on any iPhone or iPad signed in to your Apple ID.
  2. Tap your name at the top, then tap iCloud.
  3. Tap Manage Account Storage (or Manage Storage), then find Moofy in the list of apps.
  4. Tap Delete Data from iCloud (or Delete Documents & Data) and confirm.
This removes Moofy's synced copy from your private iCloud database. If Moofy is still installed on any device, that device's local copy is separate and should also be cleared using Option 1.

Still have questions?

We can't delete data on your behalf — we simply don't have access to it — but we're glad to help you find the right option above, or answer anything else about how your data works.

What gets deleted, and when

Deleted immediately

  • All transactions, accounts, categories, and budgets
  • Savings goals and their contribution history
  • Reminders and your display name / preferences
  • Receipt photos attached to transactions

Retained

  • Nothing — Moofy has no server, so there is no separate copy of your data left anywhere after you delete it locally and from iCloud.
Timeframe: deletion via the app is instant. iCloud sync of that deletion to any other signed-in device typically completes within a few minutes, depending on connectivity.
