Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Moofy ("Moofy", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you use our mobile application (the "App") and this companion website (the "Site"), collectively the "Service."

This policy is written to be transparent about what happens with your information, regardless of which specific features of Moofy you use. If a section doesn't apply to a particular feature you're using, it simply means we don't collect that category of data for that feature.

In short: we collect only what's necessary to provide and improve the Service, we don't sell your personal data, and you can request access to or deletion of your data at any time.

Data we collect

Moofy is built to collect as little as possible — in fact, the App itself sends us nothing automatically. There's no account to create, no analytics SDK, no crash reporter, and no location tracking. The only information that ever reaches us is what you choose to send directly:

  • Content you create — transactions, budgets, accounts, and goals stay on your device and, if enabled, in your own private iCloud account. We never receive a copy of this.
  • Communications — if you email support or use the in-app contact/bug-report flow, we see your email address, whatever message you write, and — only if you choose to include it — basic diagnostic info like your app version and device model.

How we use your data

Since the only data we ever receive is what you send us directly (like a support email), we use it for exactly one purpose: to respond to you. We don't use it to build profiles, target ads, train AI models, or sell it to anyone — there's no data broker or advertiser we could sell it to even if we wanted to.

Storage & security

Your app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework and, if you enable sync, in your own private iCloud database via Apple's CloudKit — encrypted in transit and at rest under Apple's standard security model. We have no server of our own, so there's no separate copy of your app data for us — or anyone else — to secure or lose.

Any support emails you send us are stored in our email provider's systems with their standard security practices. No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, but Moofy's architecture means there's very little of yours for anything to go wrong with in the first place.

Third-party services

Moofy uses no third-party services, SDKs, or vendors of any kind — no analytics provider, no crash-reporting tool, no advertising network, no customer-support platform. The only "third party" involved at all is Apple: the App relies on Apple's own frameworks (CloudKit for sync, local notifications for reminders, StoreKit for the App Store review prompt), all governed by Apple's own privacy practices for their platform services.

Analytics

Moofy has no analytics of any kind. We have no way of knowing which features you use, how often you open the app, or anything else about how you use it. There's nothing to opt out of, because nothing is ever measured.

Advertising

Moofy has no advertising, and never will. It's a one-time purchase, not an ad-supported app — there's no advertising SDK, no ad identifiers, and nothing being monetized about your attention or data.

Cookies (website)

This website may use a small number of essential cookies or similar local-storage technologies to remember basic preferences (such as whether you've dismissed a banner) and, if analytics are enabled, to understand aggregate site traffic. We do not use cookies to serve third-party advertising on this Site.

Data retention & deletion

Since Moofy has no server of its own, your data is retained for exactly as long as you keep it on your device (and, optionally, in your own private iCloud account) — nothing is retained anywhere else. There is no separate copy for us to hold onto, and no account for us to close on your behalf.

For step-by-step instructions on permanently erasing your data, see our Delete Data page.

Your rights

Because Moofy has no server and we have no access to your data, the rights you'd normally ask a company to exercise on your behalf are instead things you already have direct control over, on your own device:

  • Access — your data is already on your device; view it any time in the app.
  • Correction — edit any transaction, budget, or goal directly in the app.
  • Portability — export a full copy of your data as CSV or PDF from Settings → Export Data.
  • Deletion — permanently erase everything via Settings → Delete All Data (see our Delete Data page).

If you have questions about any of this, contact us using the details in the Contact us section below — though note we won't be able to access, change, or delete your data ourselves, since we don't have it.

Children's privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction), and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

International data transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than the one you live in. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards (such as standard contractual clauses) to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection wherever it's processed.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we'll notify you through the App or by other reasonable means before the change takes effect. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
