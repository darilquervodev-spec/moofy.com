Troubleshooting

Common issues & fixes

Try these first — most issues are resolved in under a minute.

Force-quit the app and reopen it. If that doesn't help, restart your device, then check the App Store or Google Play for a pending update. If the app still won't open, contact support with your device model and OS version so we can investigate.
Confirm both devices are connected to the internet and signed in to the same account. Pull down on the main screen to force a manual refresh. Sync can occasionally take a few minutes — if it's been longer than that, try signing out and back in.
Moofy has no separate password — App Lock uses your device's own Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode. If Face ID/Touch ID isn't working, iOS automatically falls back to your device passcode. If you're still stuck, unlocking is handled entirely by iOS, so try restarting your device; contact support if that doesn't help.
Moofy is a single one-time purchase, not a subscription or in-app unlock — there's nothing to "restore." Check your purchase history under your Apple ID (Settings → [your name] → Media & Purchases) to confirm the purchase went through, then try downloading again from the App Store. If it still won't download, contact support and we'll help investigate.
Check that notifications are enabled for the app in your device's system Settings, and that they're also turned on inside the app's own notification preferences.
Moofy has no accounts — visit our Delete Data page for step-by-step instructions on erasing everything directly in the app.
Use the contact form above or email us directly at support@moofy.app — include your device model, OS version, and app version so we can help as quickly as possible.
